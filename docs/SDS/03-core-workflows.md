# Core Workflows

## Trip Planning

1. Member creates trip.
2. Member selects cave and landowner.
3. Member sets trip date, visibility, capacity, waiver template, and callout time.
4. System creates signup/invite links.
5. Participants register and sign waivers.
6. Trip leader finalizes participant list.
7. System generates completed waiver and emails permanent view link.

## Trip Day

1. Trip leader marks trip active or in cave.
2. Trip leader updates callout status as needed.
3. Trip leader marks everyone out safely, delayed but OK, or emergency.
4. Emergency status generates restricted rescue packet and sends configured alerts.

## Trip Report

1. Trip leader submits final trip report.
2. Members may add notes.
3. Cave and landowner history are updated.
