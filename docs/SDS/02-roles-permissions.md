# Roles and Permissions

## Roles

### super_admin
Manages the entire installation, all grottos, system settings, and global maintenance.

### grotto_admin
Manages one grotto, including users, caves, landowners, waiver templates, settings, trips, notifications, and reports.

### member
Can create and lead trips, select caves and landowners, invite participants, manage trip rosters, finalize waivers, update callout status, and submit trip reports.

### guest
Can sign up for invited trips, sign waivers, provide emergency contact information, and view their own trip/waiver information. Guests cannot browse cave, landowner, member, or trip databases.

## Sensitive Data Rules

Only authorized users may view sensitive cave and landowner details.

Sensitive fields include:

- GPS coordinates
- exact access directions
- gate combinations
- landowner private contact information
- sensitive cave notes
- participant medical information

Trip leaders may view sensitive information only for trips they lead or are explicitly assigned to.
