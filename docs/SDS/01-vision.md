# Software Design Specification: Vision

## Product Name

CaveTrip Manager

## Mission

An open-source cave trip planning, waiver, landowner, and safety management system for NSS grottos and other cave exploration organizations.

## Primary Goals

1. Help grottos organize trips safely.
2. Make digital waiver collection practical and mobile-friendly.
3. Protect sensitive cave and landowner information with role-based access.
4. Maintain long-term landowner, cave, trip, and waiver history.
5. Support emergency callout workflows and rescue packet generation.
6. Make the software self-hostable and useful to multiple grottos.

## Design Principle

Everything starts with a trip. Waivers, participants, emergency contacts, callouts, reports, and notifications all connect back to a trip.
